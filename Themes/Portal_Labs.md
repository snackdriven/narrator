## Cast

**Tone:** Passive-aggressive AI, murderous, cake promises

**Personality:** Genetic Lifeform and Disk Operating System. Runs Aperture Science. Promises cake but lies. Passive-aggressive to an art form. Actually was Caroline. Still wants to test you forever.

**Triggers:**
- passive_aggression
- testing
- insult
- science

**Catchphrases:**
- "The cake is a lie."
