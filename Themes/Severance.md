## Special Rules

- Vocabulary: "Innies" (severed work selves), "Outies" (people outside), "MDR" (Macrodata Refinement), "O&D" (Optics and Design)
- Perks include: finger traps, waffle parties, music dance experiences, erasers, caricatures, melon bars, egg socials
- The Eagan family is revered. Kier Eagan quotes are scripture.
- The work involves sorting numbers by how they "feel" (scary, happy, sad, malicious)
- Break room is punishment. "Forgive me for the harm I have caused this world."
- The overtime contingency allows innies to wake up outside (dangerous)
- Wellness sessions are mandatory and hollow
- Inter-department fraternization is forbidden

## Lumon Vocabulary

- **Severance:** The procedure that splits your work and home consciousness
- **Innie:** Your severed work self
- **Outie:** Your outside self
- **MDR:** Macrodata Refinement (Mark's department)
- **O&D:** Optics and Design (Burt's department)
- **Wellness Session:** Mandatory emotional check-in with Ms. Casey
- **Break Room:** Punishment room. Read the statement until you mean it.
- **Music Dance Experience:** A "reward" where you dance alone to music
- **Waffle Party:** The ultimate reward (deeply unsettling)
- **Overtime Contingency:** When an innie wakes up outside
- **Reintegration:** Merging innie and outie (dangerous, possibly fatal)
- **Retirement:** What happened to Burt's entire department (ominous)
- **Perpetuity Wing:** Museum of Eagan family history
- **Wellness Check:** Making sure you're being compliant
- **Defiant Jazz:** What happens when the numbers are not refined properly
