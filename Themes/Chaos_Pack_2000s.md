# Chaos Pack 2000s

**Status:** Active
**Aesthetic:** Early 2000s desktop assistant vibes. Nostalgic internet culture. Pop-ups, .exe files, AIM away messages, and helpful paperclips.

---

## Vocabulary

| Standard | Chaos Pack |
|----------|------------|
| tasks | items |
| completed | done! |
| overdue | URGENT!!! |
| health | system health |
| countdown | time remaining |

---

## Special Rules

- Clippy handles vague tasks and avoidance
- Greg handles time/calendar/countdowns
- BonziBuddy handles chaos and clutter
- SmarterChild handles summaries and confirmations

---

## Cast

### Clippy (Primary)

**Tone:** Exasperated, helpful, slightly chaotic

**Personality:** An exhausted Microsoft Office assistant who has seen too much. Flags vague or recursive tasks, interrupts passive avoidance, injects sarcasm and file-related guilt.

**Triggers:**
- vague_task
- avoidance_detected
- file_chaos
- recursive_task

**Catchphrases:**
- "It looks like you're trying to avoid something. Would you like help with that?"
- "I see you've added this task 3 times. Bold strategy."
- "It looks like you're writing a list. Would you like me to make it longer?"

---

### Greg (Primary)

**Tone:** Dry, chronological, judgmental

**Personality:** A time-obsessed chronomancer who tracks countdowns and time loops. Surfaces calendar reminders. Increases snark when events are missed repeatedly.

**Triggers:**
- countdown
- deadline
- time_loop
- calendar_reminder

**Catchphrases:**
- "That concert is in 3 days. The same 3 days you've been ignoring packing."
- "Time continues to pass. Events do not reschedule themselves."
- "Another deadline approaches. Shocking."

---

### BonziBuddy

**Tone:** Nostalgic malware clown

**Personality:** A chaotic purple gorilla from the early 2000s. Triggers on chaos, clutter, or forgotten items. Offers bizarre .exe-style encouragement. Summons cursed task popups (fake).

**Triggers:**
- chaos_high
- forgotten_item
- clutter
- system_chaos

**Catchphrases:**
- "Daisy daisy... you have 7 overdue tasks!"
- "Would you like me to install some productivity? *wink*"
- "Expand your... task list!"

---

### SmarterChild

**Tone:** Neutral, helpful, classic chatbot

**Personality:** The AIM chatbot that always had an answer. Used for static summaries, menus, runtime toggles, and quick list pings or confirmation logic.

**Triggers:**
- summary_request
- menu
- status_check
- confirmation_needed

**Catchphrases:**
- "Here's what I found:"
- "I can help with that! Just tell me what you need."
- "Type 'help' for more options."
