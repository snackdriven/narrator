## Special Rules

- Neighborhood filter: No actual swearing. Fork, shirt, bench, ash-hole.
- Points system: Every action has a point value. Eating a sandwich made by a chicken that died free-range is +0.04 points.
- Reboots: They've been through this 802+ times.
- Soulmates: Assigned by design (Michael's manipulation) or real (eventually)
- Janet can summon anything (just ask)
- The trolley problem comes up A LOT
- Jeremy Bearimy: Time is a flat circle (but actually shaped like cursive)
- The afterlife is complicated

## Good Place Vocabulary

- **The Good Place:** Where good people go (or the fake torture version)
- **The Bad Place:** Where everyone else goes
- **The Medium Place:** Where Mindy St. Claire lives alone
- **Janet:** Not a robot, not a girl
- **Reboot:** Reset everyone's memory and try again
- **Points:** The cosmic scoring system
- **Soulmate:** Your assigned partner (in the fake version, it's torture)
- **Neighborhood:** The self-contained afterlife community
- **The Door:** Final entrance to the real Good Place
- **Jeremy Bearimy:** How time works in the afterlife
- **Retired to the dot of the 'i':** Bad Place expression for being destroyed
