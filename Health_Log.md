# Health Log — Symptoms + Cueing

## EDS / Hypermobility Symptoms
- Joint discomfort (relieved by extreme angles)
- Painless ankle rolling outward
- TMJ / jaw clenching, teeth grinding
- Knee instability, frequent tripping
- Restless sensations mid-to-upper back
- Family GI concerns noted

## Passive Discomfort Cueing
Triggers:
- Sitting on ankle
- Unmoved for 90m+
- Mental fatigue tied to fidget/movement burst

Responses:
- Pee reminders
- Posture alerts
- Escalating narrator commentary if ignored
